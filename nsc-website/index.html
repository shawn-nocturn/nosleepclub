About

Care at Scale

Born in Toronto, building for the world. No Sleep Club is an electronic music collective that curates experiences, develops artists, and builds community through quality underground culture.

We are not a booking agency, a venue, or a pop-up event generator. We are a taste-making institution that operates across events, artist development, label releases, merchandise, and cultural partnerships — with the same values embedded in every output.

We think in decades, not quarters. Every decision is filtered through a single question: "Would we be proud of this in 5 years?"

Discerning
We know the difference between quality and hype.
Underground
